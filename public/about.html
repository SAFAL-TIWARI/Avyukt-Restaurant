Our Story

Established in 2020

Located in the heart of Durga Nagar, Vidisha, Avyukt Restaurant has been serving delightful culinary experiences since 2020. We are a pure vegetarian establishment dedicated to bringing you authentic flavors across a diverse range of cuisines, including Indian, Chinese, Continental, Punjabi, and South Indian delicacies.

Whether you are looking for a hearty breakfast, a cozy dine-in experience, or a special candlelight dinner under the stars with our rooftop seating and live music, Avyukt Restaurant is the perfect destination for every occasion.
